If you are a Shipper...

Request

Send your shipment requirement ( Product, Delivery date and time) to our system.

Match

We will match your shipment with our reliable carriers at an earliest possible departure from airport Free Zone.

Deliver

We manage to move your shipment with carrier to ensure your product deliver to customer safety and on time.

Save

We offer a competitive price, averaging 30% lower than market price for the same route. We aim to lowering your transportation cost and time spent managing transportation.

If you are a Carrier...

Notify

Upload your backhaul truck and route to our system. We will utilize your backhaul trip.

Match

We will match your backhaul truck and route with our shippers requirement.

Sucess

Instead of return empty, your backhaul trip is full load, off setting your operation costs.
